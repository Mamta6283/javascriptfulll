Can TEDx Talk help your business?

  • Report this article
Bilyana Georgieva

Bilyana Georgieva

Digital Transformation Director | Program Manager | LinkedIn Top Voice Change Management | Public Speaker

Published Nov 3, 2021
+ Follow

By Bilyana Georgieva, TEDx Coach, Digital Nerd and Multi-Award Winning Professional Speaker, founder of The 'Soon-To-Be a TEDx Speaker' Program. 

You will hear me often saying

Whats the point of doing a TEDx if nobody ever finds it on YouTube?

The 'Soon-To-Be a TEDx Speaker' newsletter aims to give you tips so you can have a successful TEDx Journey. Make sure you also join me for my weekly LIVE Podcast every Friday at 12 pm GMT / 8 am EDT / 5 am PT. It's your time to Ask Me Anything about TED and TEDx talks.


How many times you've been told to do a TEDx Talk?

May be you've heard someone saying that becoming a TEDx Speaker will give you a huge boost on everything you do, business-wise and personally.

That's great, but what exactly is the opportunity there for you?

People do a TEDx talk to share their wisdom, knowledge, know-how or rear projects that no one ever heard about.

They want to inspire others because they know their personal story is powerful and will change other people's lives.

Yet, not many understand the full power of a TEDx talk, when done with the right intention.

I don't want you to listen to other people's words and blindly believe.

I want you to know the facts and use them in full advantage for yourself.


Here are The 5 Super Powers of TED and TEDx talks:

Nr. 1 VISIBILITY

Each Talk is published automatically on 50+ platforms, including:

  • TED.com, a platform that has 2.2 billion views,
  • YouTube, the biggest video platform with 2.3 billion users worldwide,
  • Netflix, which has 207.64 million subscribers, and
  • Spotify, currently having 155 million subscribers

The TED platform itself summarises the number of all views from all platforms. Thats why the views number there is different from those on YouTube for example. Have you noticed that before?

Now read the amount of users and subscribers on the 4 platforms I mentioned. I'm not trying to scare you but to realise the full power of a TEDx Talk.

If making yourself visible or having a goal your business to become internationally well-known and recognised, then doing a TEDx must be part of your marketing and sales plan.

The best thing here is that you don't do anything to reach all the 50+ platforms. The TED Headquarter is doing it for you


Nr 2. POSITION AS AN EXPERT IN YOUR FIELD and GAIN CREDIBILITY AND AUTHORITY

We all know that TED and the locally organised TEDx events are ranked as #1 stage in the world. There is no other stage yet, that has the same scale and impact as TED and TEDx.

The moment you announce that you became a TEDx Speaker people will look up and see you as an Authority. It happens with every speaker.

If thousands of people watch and share your talk you will know that the wisdom you shared is something never seen before, your lesson is well accepted and now you get the Credibility you always wanted to have.

Nr 3. TEDx IS A LEGACY

TEDx talks and books are the fastest way to create and leave a Legacy behind in 21st century.

Have you ever thought about that?

No pressure on you, but I want you to also realised that once your TEDx talk is published, it will be on all the 50+ platforms and will exist until the last platform exist. I recon that will be a long period of time!

Therefore your message to the world turns into a powerful Legacy


Nr 4. BECOME AN INFLUENCER

Becoming an Influencer comes together with the title TEDx Speaker and is a bit like people seeing you as an Authority. It just comes with the title. Don't be surprise when strangers send you private messages or people recognise you on the street or in the supermarket. It happened to me and to a lot of my clients. You will become 'that person from the screen' for many people.


Nr 5. TEDx IS A POTENT DIGITAL BUSINESS CARD

The world changed drastically in the past 2 years. People want to see the human side in someone before they decide to work with her or him. That goes to all senior levels, all business owners and everyone that run a business or lead a team nowadays.

In the past people were afraid to share personal stories, thinking this will deposition them. Today is exactly the opposite and the best way to show that human side of you is by doing a TEDx Talk.

It's digital and accessible by everyone.

It's easy to share and I highly recommend you to always share the link to your YouTube TEDx video. I'll write another article on why this is super important for your success.

People remember stories much easier than financial figures and business results, simply because they relate to what you went through. They will have huge respect for having the gut to stand up and sharing it openly with the world

Hence a TEDx talk will help you to touch other people's hearts and minds and will open doors that were closed for you before. I've seen it so many times


Each of those 5 Super Powers of TED and TEDx talks is a potential TEDx Goal for you.

Before you start writing a script and practising your speech make sure you are very clear on what outcome you expect from your own TEDx talk?

Similar to business you should write down your goal as otherwise you will not know if your talk is successful or not.

Every time I present the 5 powers and ask future TEDx speakers to write their TEDx goals, they chose all of them. Thats ok if you did that too. Just make sure you turn them into a TANGIBLE goal.

For example if Visibility is your TEDx Goal a possible tangible value would be to reach 100,000 views on YouTube. Im totally making it up but you got the idea.

Have fun writing your TEDx goal !

Share it in the comment below or send me a DM. I also want to know if you learn something new and practical today?
